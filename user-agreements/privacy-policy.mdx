**1. Introduction**

Welcome to Covert! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our Discord bot. By using Covert, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

***

**2. Information We Collect**

Covert collects various types of information, including:

* **User Data:** We collect information such as usernames, user IDs, and any content you choose to share with the bot.

* **Server Data:** We may collect information about the servers (guilds) where Covert is added, including server IDs and channel IDs.

* **Interaction Data:** We log interactions with the bot, including commands issued and responses provided.

***

**3. How We Use Your Information**

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

* **Functionality:** To operate and provide the services and features of Covert.

* **Improvement:** To analyze usage patterns and improve the bot's performance and functionality.

* **Support:** To offer support and respond to inquiries or issues related to the bot.

***

**4. Data Retention**

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy. Data may be deleted upon request or if it is no longer needed for operational purposes.

***

**5. Data Security**

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

***

**6. Data Sharing and Disclosure**

We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

* **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.

* **Service Providers:** We may use third-party services to help with bot operations. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes for which it was disclosed.

***

**7. User Rights**

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us through [Discord](https://discord.gg/ZAkGUuYS3B).

***

**8. Changes to This Privacy Policy**

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here, and the updated policy will take effect immediately upon posting. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
